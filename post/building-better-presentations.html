Building better presentations

Mar 11, 2021 · 1 min read

← Back to Blog

Slide decks are everywhere. Like it or not, they are the standard format for business communication. Why not get good at telling a story in this medium?

I recently ran a training on how to build effective and attractive presentations.

In the presentation, I go into length about:

  • Creating story using "situation," "complication," "resolution"
  • Writing compelling content that is specific, novel, and interesting
  • Using standard page templates for specific purposes
  • De-wording bullets to make them short and punchy
  • Building attractive pages by paying attention to white space, etc.
  • Making presentations better and faster with a few other tips and tricks
