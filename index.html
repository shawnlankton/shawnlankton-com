Quick Bio

I get excited about helping and building. As an executive, I aspire to help customers and teams achieve more while building great culture and scalable businesses. I'm an engineer at heart and constantly pushing to go faster.

I'm an experienced CEO and Tech Executive. I've held leadership roles at various scale-up businesses and co-founded McKinsey's Growth Stage Tech practice. Accelerating the growth of technology-driven business has been a passion since completing my PhD in Electrical Engineering at Georgia Tech.

I now live in Atlanta and spend as much time as possible with my family.
